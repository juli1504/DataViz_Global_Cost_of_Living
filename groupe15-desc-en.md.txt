# EcoPulse: Global Economy Viz

**Project Link:** [https://economypulse.netlify.app/](https://economypulse.netlify.app/)

EcoPulse is an interactive dashboard analyzing 50 years of global economic data (1970-2021).
It features a custom radial visualization ("Economic Pulse") to explore GDP structure, trade flows, and sectoral distribution, along with comparative tools for multi-country analysis.
